### Market Glossary

**Key Terms:**
- **Basis** - Difference between futures and spot prices
- **Contango** - Futures prices higher than spot prices
- **Backwardation** - Futures prices lower than spot prices
- **Carry** - Cost of holding physical commodity
- **Spread** - Price difference between related contracts
- **Volatility** - Measure of price fluctuations
- **Open Interest** - Number of outstanding contracts
